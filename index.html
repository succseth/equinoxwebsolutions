Who We Are

Built for the businesses that keep communities running.

Equinox Web Solutions is a modern web design agency helping small businesses across the United States establish a professional online presence without the complexity or upfront cost that usually comes with it.

We specialise in clean, fast, mobile-friendly websites for local service businesses. Plumbers, salons, restaurants, contractors, and everything in between. If your customers need to find you online, we make sure they can.

Our model is simple: we build your website for free so you can see exactly what you get before committing to anything. Ongoing hosting, maintenance, and updates are then handled through an affordable monthly plan that fits comfortably into any business budget.
